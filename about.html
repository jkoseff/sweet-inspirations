About Sweet Inspirations

Beth Garcia and Kim Ahlenslager first crossed paths as college students, but soon learned of a shared appetite for creating mouthwatering baked goods and decadent candies. The bond turned into a lifelong friendship beyond the boundaries of their kitchen. Upon countless encouragement from friends and family, Beth and Kim's friendship turned into a thriving successful business.

In 2000, Sweet Inspirations was created, providing quality candies that are all individually handmade from the finest ingredients. The truffles are made in a variety of flavors and can be individually wrapped or boxed to appeal to the recipients love for chocolate!

Celebrate the holidays, weddings, parties or simply to just say "hello" by sending a Sweet Inspiration truffle or candy!
