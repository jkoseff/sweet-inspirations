Welcome

Sweet Inspirations are the creation of longtime friends, Beth Garcia and Kim Ahlenslager. What started as Beth's childhood love for her "Easy-bake Oven" and Kim's passion to explore and create in the kitchen as a small child, became the launching pad that would soon transform into the motivation behind Sweet Inspirations.
